## Description

AI assisted passion project exploring how to bridge part of the gap between retail strategy development and quantitative research. I used a familiar MNQ opening range breakout as a case study, then built a Python research workflow around costs, lookahead controls, purged out of sample validation, permutation and multiple testing controls, Monte Carlo resampling, execution stress, and parameter stability. The public Streamlit app presents reviewed aggregate evidence, rejected ideas, and open limitations without exposing raw licensed data or trade level records. The June 2021 through June 2026 historical sample contains 729 cost adjusted trades. This remains a work in progress, with paper forward testing and pre 2021 validation still outstanding. AI accelerated coding, documentation, and interface iteration; I retained responsibility for the research design, evidence boundary, and published claims. Research and engineering demonstration only.
